Interview Framework — 10 Stages

The most rigorous hiring process in the industry. Ten stages, each testing different competencies.

Stage 1: Recruiter Screen

30 min · Culture, comp, role understanding

Can they articulate career arc in 3 minutes? Do they understand the full-stack scope?

Stage 2: Leadership Screening

45 min · Portfolio walkthrough, leadership philosophy

Do they lead with impact or activity? Can they speak to business outcomes?

Stage 3: Product Thinking

60 min · Strategy challenge — SPADE framework

Given a business problem: define strategy, users, metrics, MVP, roadmap in 30 minutes.

Stage 4: UX Architecture

90 min · Portfolio deep dive with hostile questions

Defend every decision with evidence. Show messy process. Quantify impact.

Stage 5: Systems Thinking

60 min · Whiteboard design system architecture

Token structure, component hierarchy, governance, React implementation.

Stage 6: AI Strategy

45 min · Design AI-powered feature from scratch

Human-AI interaction, trust, transparency, feedback loops, ethics.

Stage 7: Design Exercise

2hr take-home + 60 min live session

IA, flows, wireframes, hi-fi screen. Live: walk through work, iterate on feedback.

Stage 8: Technical Evaluation

60 min · React code review + pair programming

Identify a11y, performance, design-system violations. Pair-program a fix.

Stage 9: Executive Presentation

45 min · Present UX transformation proposal

Vision, roadmap, team, metrics, investment ask. Board-room readiness test.

Stage 10: CEO / CXO Bar-Raiser

45 min · Vision, philosophy, culture fit

Where is design headed in 5 years? How does AI change the process? Why this company?

Preparation Roadmap

Phased preparation: from strong senior to unstoppable principal-level candidate.

30-Day Sprint — "Build the Foundation"

Week 1–2: Portfolio Surgery
Rebuild 3 case studies using IMPACT. Every case: research → design → metrics. Include messy process. Record before/after. Build narrative arc.
Week 3–4: Skills Gap Audit
Self-assess against 6 domains. Score honestly. Identify 2 domains below level 3. Begin daily: 1 product exercise, 1 code review, 1 research deep-dive.

90-Day Campaign — "Sharpen the Edge"

Month 2: Technical Depth
Build production React component library (10+ components, Storybook, TypeScript, a11y). Review 10 open-source design systems. Ship to GitHub.
Month 3: Interview Machine
10 mock interviews (1 per stage type). Record, watch, fix. SPADE practice. 15 STAR-A stories. Deep company research.

180-Day Program — "Build the Brand"

Month 4–5: Thought Leadership
4 LinkedIn articles. 1 local talk. 1 open-source contribution. Case study blog. 2 conference submissions. Public learning resource.
Month 6: Active Pursuit
10 target roles simultaneously. Warm introductions. Custom portfolio per company. Multiple offers → strong negotiation. 30-60-90 plan ready.
