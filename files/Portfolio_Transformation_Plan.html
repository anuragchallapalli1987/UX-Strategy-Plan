Visual Design Standard for Case Studies

  • Color palette: Dark background (#0C0E13 or #111827), gold/amber accents, clean white text. Matches enterprise/architect aesthetic — NOT a playful designer portfolio
  • Typography: Use a serif for headings (Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond) + DM Sans for body. Signals sophistication and strategic thinking
  • Layout: Full-width sections with generous whitespace. Architecture diagrams should be custom-made, not screenshots of sticky notes
  • Consistency: All 7 projects should share the same visual template. This itself demonstrates systems thinking
